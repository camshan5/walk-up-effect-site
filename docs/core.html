Walk Up
At Its Core

We design and build coworking spaces tailored to the needs of growing teams.

“Walk Up”…what does that actually mean. In a sense, you can take it quite literally and define it as walking up to someone or something. And while that may be true, our one word answer to “what does walk up mean”, is COURAGE. Walking up means that someone has the courage to do 1 of 2 things.

1. Having the courage to walk up to someone who may be struggling with something. Walking up to them and taking the 6 walk up steps to ensure that they have a sense of hope and strength to continue moving forward.

2. Having the courage to walk up to someone and being vulnerable enough to express that you are struggling with mental health. Letting someone know that you have anxiety, depression, or suicidal thoughts. Its having the courage to step out of your comfort zone and the strength to express your struggles openly. Its having the courage to SHARE YOUR STORY!
