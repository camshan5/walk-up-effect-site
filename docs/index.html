What are

Inspire

The main goal of this program is to encourage and inspire individuals to Walk Up.

Walk Up means to have the courage to...

  • Walk up to someone who you see that may be struggling and offer support.
  • Walk up to someone and ask for support if you are struggling with your mental health and well-being.

Provide

Only 20% of those struggling with their mental health get the proper treatment.

Our goal is to increase that percentage and be able to provide therapy/treatment to any individual who needs it. We understand that therapy can be expensive.

With our program and our fundraising platform, we are able to provide the funds to any student who needs therapy and treatment to make sure they get they care they deserve.

Motivate

We believe in the power of story and we believe it is tough to judge someone when you know their story.

When a struggling individual hears someone share their story on mental health issues and how they continue to overcome it, they too may feel motivated to speak up and share their story which in turn can motivate more and more people.

With our program and fundraising platform, your school will be provided with an amazing motivational speaker that will share their powerful mental health story to increase strength and inspire individuals to walk up.

Change

Our mission is to change the way everyone perceives mental health.

We want to normalize the conversation behind mental health, encourage people to walk up, and empower individuals to speak up. We believe by sticking to our mission daily, we will be able to change the lives of many students and provide them the daily hope they need.

How to Walk Up

Take these Walk Up Steps to begin the process of elevating yourself or someone else.

Heal others

Your role. Your job is simple. Walk Up, talk, and learn. Remember theyre waiting for you.

Result. When you Walk Up to someone with the two Cs, Courage and Care, you will have left a lasting impression that can provide an individual with much needed hope and strength.

1

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle.

2

Communicate

Let them feel "seen". Talk to them or give a smile.

3

Learn

What's their story? What can you do to support them?

4

Imagine

What would you want if roles were reversed?

5

Comfort

Make sure they feel a sense of hope by the time you leave.

6

Follow Up

Make an effort for you to see them again. Provide hope.

Heal yourself

Your role. Be courageous. Know that you are not alone. Walk Up and express your struggles.

Result. Asking for help shows strength and can encourage others around you to do the same. When you ask for help you are starting your journey to living a happier life.

1

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle that you may be facing.

2

Be Humble

Its ok to have a struggle and its ok to ask for support.

3

Ask for Help

Be courageous. Walk Up to someone for help.

4

Listen

Listen to their advice.

5

Take Action

Make time in each day to work on yourself.

6

Surround

Surround yourself with people that support you.
