How to Walk Up

Take these Walk Up steps to begin the process of elevating yourself or someone else.

Heal Others Heal Yourself

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle.

Communicate

Let them feel "seen". Talk to them or give a smile.

Learn

What's their story? What can you do to help?

Imagine

What would you want or need if roles were reversed?

Comfort

Make sure they feel a sense of hope by the time you leave.

Follow Up

Make an effort for you to see or talk to them again. Provide hope.

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle that you may be facing.

Be Humble

Admit to yourself that you have a struggle and need help.

Ask for Help

Be courageous. Walk up to somebody to ask for help.

Listen

Listen to their advice.

Take Action

Make time in each day to work on yourself.

Surround

Surround yourself with people that will support and help you.
