Our Mission & Goals

To normalize the conversation behind mental health

Mission

Walk Up is a do-good company with a mission to inspire individuals to be courageous and walk up to be the help, the smile, and the support an individual may need. We have a goal to normalize and spark the conversation behind mental health and to encourage individuals to talk and share their struggles and story with others. We want everyone to feel comfortable asking for help.

Goals

Our main goal is to impact. We hope to leave an impact on the staff, the students, and the parents. It is important to us that every student we come into contact with feels inspired to help others, has the strength to get through another day, and wants to be the change we all want to see in the world.

Number 1

Inspire students to be courageous and Walk Up to someone who appears to be struggling.

Number 3

Reach those students who are afraid to ask for help. We want to provide them with the strength, love and support they need to get through each day.

Number 2

Educate students on mental health. Teach them the signs, symptoms, causes, and ways to cope with mental health struggles.

Number 4

We want to provide the school with consistent motivation and inspiration by bringing guest speakers onto camput to talk about mental health, hope, and healing.
