Our Mission & Goals

Find out more about our journey to normalize the conversation behind mental health

Our Mission Our Goal

We are a do-good company.

Walk Up's mission has always been to inspire. We strive to inspire individuals to be courageous and walk up and be the help someone may, the smile, and the support a person may need.

We were founded with the goal to normalize and spark the conversation behind mental health and encourage people to talk and share their struggles and story with others. We want everyone to feel comfortable asking for help.

Our primary goal: Impact.

We hope to make an impact on every staff member, student, and parent that takes part in our program. It is important to us that every student we come into contact with feels inspired to help others, has the strength to get through another day, and wants to be the change we need in the world.

Our focus is to...

Inspire students to be courageous and Walk Up to someone who appears to be struggling.

Educate students on mental health. Teach the signs, symptoms, causes, and ways to cope with mental health struggles.

Reach the students who are afraid to ask for help and provide them with the strength, love and support they need to get through each day.

Provide the school with motivation and inspiration by bringing guest speakers to talk about mental health, hope, and healing.
