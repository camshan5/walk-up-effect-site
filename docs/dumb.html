Educate

Throughout our program, students and staff will be educated on all things mental health.

What are

These are all things that are very important for everyone to understand to make sure that everyone is on the same page and everyone can recognize the symptoms of someone who may be struggling.

Inspire

The main goal of this program is to encourage and inspire individuals to Walk Up.

Walk Up means to have the courage to...

  1. Walk up to someone who you see that may be struggling and offer help.
  2. Walk up to someone and ask for help is you are experiencing issues with your mental health and well-being.

Provide

Only 20% of those struggling with their mental health get the proper treatment.

Our goal is to increase that percentage and be able to provide therapy/treatment to any individual who needs it. We understand that therapy can be expensive.

With our program and our fundraising platform, we are able to provide the funds to any student who needs it to make sure they get they care they deserve.

Motivate

We believe in the power of story and we believe it is tough to judge someone when you know their story.

When a struggling individual hears someone share their story on mental health issues and how they overcame it, they too may feel motivated to speak up and share their story which in turns can motivate more and more people.

With our program and fundraising platform, your school will be provided with an amazing motivational speaker that will share their powerful mental health story.

Change

Our mission is to change the way everyone perceives mental health.

We want to normalize the conversation behind it, encourage people to walk up, and empower individuals to speak up. We believe by sticking to our mission daily, we will be able to change the lives of many students and provide them the daily hope they need.
