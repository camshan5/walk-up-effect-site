Founder & CEO

Brandon Gross

Brandon was born and raised in Southern California, where he quickly developed a love of sports and water. Fast forward several years, Brandon is playing collegiate water polo at California Lutheran University in Thousand Oaks, CA. During his time in college, Brandon began coaching youth water polo and found a new passion; teaching.

Upon graduation, he jumped into the education world and became a 7th-grade algebra teacher and athletic director. After a few years of teaching, Brandon took a job as a director at Apex Leadership Co, a company that contracts with schools throughout the United States to teach a unique program focused on valuable leadership skills for students. Additionally, Apex Leadership offers a service that utilizes its staff and the students who have taken the leadership course to assist the school in raising contributions from the families and community for the school.

Brandon has a love for helping others be their best selves and reach their full potential. While on his honeymoon, Brandon received the heartbreaking news of a mass shooting at a bar in the town he attended college. This news led Brandon to develop the idea of
The Walk Up Effect.

Brandon brings the vision behind the company that will help provide the youth with the resources and strength they need to overcome their mental health struggles.
