How to Walk Up

Take these Walk Up Steps to begin the process of elevating yourself or someone else.

Heal others

Your role. Your job is simple. Walk Up, talk, and learn. Remember… they’re waiting for you.

Result. When you Walk Up to someone with the two C’s, Courage and Care, you will have left a lasting impression that can provide an individual with much needed hope and strength.

1

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle.

2

Communicate

Let them feel "seen". Talk to them or give a smile.

3

Learn

What's their story? What can you do to help?

4

Imagine

What would you want if roles were reversed?

5

Comfort

Make sure they feel a sense of hope by the time you leave.

6

Follow Up

Make an effort for you to see them again. Provide hope.

Heal yourself

Your role. Be courageous. Know that you are not alone. Walk Up and express your struggles.

Result. Asking for help shows strength and can encourage others around you to do the same. When you ask for help you are starting your journey to living a happier life.

1

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle that you may be facing.

2

Be Humble

Admit to yourself that you have a struggle and need help.

3

Ask for Help

Be courageous. Walk Up to someone for help.

4

Listen

Listen to their advice.

5

Take Action

Make time in each day to work on yourself.

6

Surround

Surround yourself with people that support you.
